About Me

Graphic Designer!

Greetings! I'm Gusha, a driven and enthusiastic graphic designer currently studying at JIHC College. At 17, I'm fueled by curiosity and a passion for creating visuals that leave a lasting impression. While my formal education in IT at JIHC College provides a strong foundation, my true calling lies in graphic design. Through hands-on experience, I've honed my skills in logo design, branding, and promotional materials. Notably, I've crafted the logo for XquisiteXperience, reflecting their innovative spirit and commitment to excellence. Continuously evolving, I embrace each project as an opportunity for growth. With a blend of technical prowess and creative flair, I'm dedicated to pushing the boundaries of design to deliver impactful solutions. Excited for what lies ahead, I'm confident in my ability to make a meaningful impact in the dynamic world of graphic design.
